InspireMe App

The InspireMe app is intuitive and available on IOS. In it, you can find your ideal mentor / mentee match. This thanks to the recommendation of an empathic algorithm. In addition, through a structure for monitoring objectives and tasks, you will be able to develop a career plan that will make you competitive in today's industries.

Start your mentoring program instantly!

How this works

Step 1

Find a profession:

Find details next to a video about the purpose of a particular profession. You can use different filters to find what you are looking for or discover something new.

Step 2

Find a mentor:

Within each profession you have the option to connect with a mentor who will guide you, provide support, and open doors along the way.

Step 3

Schedule a session:

Once you found a mentor that fits your needs, schedule a session to talk about the profession, define some goals and set the upcoming follow-up meetings.

Step 4

Mentor follow-up:

After the first session, follow-up guidelines are established for the student's progress in the desired career.

Learn about different careers and find work that drives you.
